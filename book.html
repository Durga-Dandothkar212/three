CHAPTER1

Chapter 1 of The Book Thief introduces Liesel, the book thief, and Death, the narrator, as she moves in with the Hubermanns and befriends Rudy Steiner. Death also introduces himself as sensitive to light and color, and regretful about collecting souls. He first sees the book thief on a train, where he collects the soul of a young boy, and the book thief watches him. Death also reminds the reader that everyone will die eventually.In The Book Thief, chapter 2 is about Christmas with the Hubermanns. Liesel receives two books for Christmas, Faust the Dog and The Lighthouse, after Hans trades his cigarette rations for them.

CHAPTER3

In part three of The Book Thief, Liesel continues to steal books. She steals The Shoulder Shrug from a pile of burning books and shows it to Hans. Hans is concerned about the book's content and the political repercussions of Liesel's actions. He asks Liesel to keep a secret for him, and she promises to do so.
